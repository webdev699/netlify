About Us

HungrySharkEvolutionDoc is an independent educational platform dedicated to documenting mobile gaming technology and software modification architecture. Our mission is to provide clear, safe, and factual information to users interested in the technical aspects of Android applications.

We are a team of tech enthusiasts and writers who believe in the importance of transparency in the software ecosystem. We analyze updates, patches, and version histories to help users understand what they are installing on their devices.

Our Core Code of Ethics:

  • Transparency: We clearly distinguish between official and unofficial software.
  • Safety: We prioritize user security and warn against potential risks of sideloading.
  • Legality: We respect intellectual property rights and comply with DMCA requests promptly.
