Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it legal to use Hungry Shark Evolution MOD APK?

A: Modifying software often violates the developer's Terms of Service and End User License Agreement (EULA). While downloading and using modified apps for personal offline usage is broadly in a legal gray area in some jurisdictions, distributing them can be copyright infringement. This website provides information for educational purposes only.

Q: Can this MOD harm my device?

A: Any file installed from outside the official Play Store carries potential risks. While reputable education sites scan files for malware, there is always a non-zero risk when sideloading. It is recommended to use antivirus software on your Android device.

Q: Why did the installation fail?

A: The most common failure (App Not Installed) occurs when the original Play Store version of the game is still installed on your device. Android cannot install two versions of the same package name with different signatures. You must uninstall the original version first.

Q: Is this the official website?

A: No. We are a documentation fan site. The official game is developed by Ubisoft and can be found on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

Q: Will my progress save to the cloud?

A: No. Modified versions generally cannot connect to official Google Play Games services for cloud saving because the app signature does not match the official developer certificate. All progress will be stored locally on your device.
